About Me

Preston :)

Hi, I’m Preston (they/them)!
I’m a graphic design student who loves bold lettering, expressive color, pastel palettes, and playful designs with personality.
Outside of design, I’m really into K-pop, anime, video games, and D&D. A lot of my work is inspired by the things I love- whether that’s bright, stylized visuals, character-driven design, or a little bit of childlike wonder!
I’m especially drawn to projects that let me experiment with color, storytelling, and unique visual identity
